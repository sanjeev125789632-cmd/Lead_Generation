## Honest caveats
- A café/operator having a Zomato, Swiggy, Instagram or OTA (Booking/Agoda) page does **not** mean it has its own website — those are exactly the businesses to pitch (they pay commissions and don't own their customer relationship).
- Google Maps list-view is unreliable for the "Website" button — that's why each entry here was opened and checked on its own profile.
- Phone numbers occasionally get flagged by Google as possibly outdated; confirm on first contact.
- Rafting operators in Rishikesh and most Manali tour agencies already have websites — low yield, skipped.
