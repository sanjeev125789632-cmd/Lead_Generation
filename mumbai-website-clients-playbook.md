# Mumbai Website Client Finder — Playbook & Outreach Kit

Goal: find local Mumbai businesses (trades/services, restaurants/cafes/retail, professionals) that have **no proper website** and pitch them one.

---

## 1. Where the "no website" businesses actually are

The best prospects are on directories but NOT on the open web with their own domain. Hunt them here:

- **JustDial** — justdial.com/Mumbai → search a category + locality. Listings show phone; many have **no website link**. That gap = your prospect.
- **Google Maps** — search "electrician Andheri", "salon Ghatkopar" etc. Open a listing: if the **Website** button is missing (only Call/Directions), it's a lead.
- **Sulekha, IndiaMART** — same logic for services and small manufacturers/traders.
- **Instagram** — businesses running everything off an IG page with a phone in bio and **no "link in bio" site** are prime, motivated buyers.
- **Zomato/Swiggy only** — a café with a Zomato page but no own site can't take direct orders or build a brand. Good angle.

### Best-converting segments (least likely to have sites, high need)
- Trades: plumbers, electricians, carpenters, painters, pest control, AC/appliance repair, packers & movers.
- Local retail/food: standalone cafés, bakeries, tiffin/catering, sweet shops, boutiques, gyms.
- Professionals: dentists/clinics, physiotherapists, salons/spas, tuition classes, CA/lawyer solo practices, real estate brokers.

### High-density Mumbai localities to work
Andheri (E/W), Bandra, Borivali, Malad, Ghatkopar, Thane, Dadar, Vile Parle, Powai, Chembur. Pick ONE locality + ONE category per session so your pitch stays hyper-relevant.

---

## 2. Qualify before you pitch (2-minute checklist)

Mark a business as a real lead only if ALL are true:
1. **No own website** — Google "[business name] Mumbai". No domain of their own (a Zomato/JustDial/Facebook page does NOT count).
2. **Reachable** — public phone or WhatsApp number listed.
3. **Active** — recent reviews/posts (open, spending money, cares about customers).
4. **Can afford it** — established storefront or steady service, not a one-person side hustle.

A dead/abandoned listing is not a lead. Skip it.

## 4. Outreach templates

### A. WhatsApp / cold message (best for Mumbai SMBs)
> Namaste [Name] ji, I came across [Business] on [JustDial/Google] — you've got great reviews but I noticed you don't have your own website yet. I build simple, mobile-friendly websites for local Mumbai businesses that help customers find you on Google and message you directly. I can make a free 1-page demo for [Business] — no obligation. Shall I send it over?

### B. Phone call opener (15 seconds)
> "Hi, am I speaking with the owner of [Business]? I help Mumbai businesses like yours get found on Google. I noticed you don't have a website — most customers check online before visiting. I can show you a quick demo I've made specifically for [Business]. Do you have two minutes, or should I WhatsApp it?"

### C. Email (if you have an address)
> **Subject:** A website for [Business] — free demo inside
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I found [Business] on [source] — strong reviews, but no website of your own. That means customers who Google you land on a directory instead of *you*, and can't see your services, photos, or contact you directly.
>
> I build clean, mobile-first websites for local Mumbai businesses that show up on Google and turn searches into calls. I'd like to build you a free one-page demo — you only pay if you love it.
>
> Reply "yes" and I'll have it ready in 48 hours.
>
> [Your name] · [phone] · [portfolio link]

### D. The demo-first close (highest conversion)
Don't sell — show. Build a free 1-page mockup with their name, photos (from their IG/Zomato), services and a WhatsApp button, then send: *"Made this for you in 20 mins — here's the link. Want me to make it live?"* Seeing their own business online closes deals faster than any pitch.

---

## 5. Pricing anchors (Mumbai SMB market)
- 1-page starter site: ₹5,000–10,000
- 3–5 page business site: ₹12,000–25,000
- + ₹2,000–5,000/yr maintenance, or a monthly retainer for updates/SEO.
Start low to build a portfolio and testimonials, then raise rates.

---

## 6. Daily routine that works
1. Pick 1 locality + 1 category.
2. Open JustDial + Google Maps, list 20 businesses with no website into the tracker.
3. Build 1–2 free demos for the most promising.
4. WhatsApp all 20 with template A.
5. Follow up non-repliers once after 3 days.

Consistency beats volume — 20 qualified messages a day for two weeks will land clients.
